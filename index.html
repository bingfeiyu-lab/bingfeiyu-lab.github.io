Our lab investigates two fundamental aspects of immune system biology: (1) the molecular mechanisms underlying inter-cellular immune recognition, by developing tools to link immune recognition and molecular programming at single-cell resolution and (2) the role of X- chromosome inactivation in immune cells, focusing on how this epigenetic regulation contributes to sex-based differences in immune responses.
