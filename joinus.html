The Yu lab at USC in LA is hiring at all levels. Reach out if you are interested!

We are recruiting postdoctoral researchers, graduate students, and research assistants with motivation and curiousity to dive into systems and synthetic immunology.

We encourage internal/external collaboration and recruit talents with diverse expertise. Candidates with one of the following backgrounds are strongly encouraged to apply

1. Immunology, Tumor immunology, T cell biology, Adoptive cell therapy
2. Synthetic Biology, Genetic and viral engineering, High-throughput screen
3. Single-cell genomics, Computational biology, Functional epigenetics

For interested postdocs, please send your CV, a cover letter with current/past work and future plan, and contact information of 3 references to Bingfei.

For interested graduate students, please apply to USC PIBBS PhD program or MMI MS program. Feel free to email Bingfei to discuss potential rotations.

For interersted research assistants, please send your CV to Bingfei. Prefered candidates should hold a bachelor degree in biology, and have previously worked in a lab.
