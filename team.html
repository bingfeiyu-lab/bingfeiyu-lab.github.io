Bingfei Yu, Ph.D.
(she/her/hers)

Principle Investigator
bingfeiy@usc.edu

Twitter

Dr. Yu is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Molecular Microbiology & Immunology and a primary member of Norris Cancer Center at Kech School of Medicine at the University of Southern California.

Bingfei received her Ph.D. in Immunology with Dr. Ananda Goldrath at the University of California, San Diego, working on differentiation pathways of T cells combating infectious diseases and cancer. During her postdoctoral research in the lab of Dr. Howard Chang at Stanford, Bingfei dived into epigenetics and single cell genomics field to develop technologies that decode immune recognition and molecular blueprints. She is a recipient of Parker Bridge Fellow award supported by Parker Institute for Cancer Immunotherapy and the V foundation.

Outside lab, Bingfei is always looking for tasty boba teas and spicy food. She loves karyoke, pop music, and badminton.
